# À TRADUIRE: About Kilo Paid Plans

À TRADUIRE: Kilo Code accelerates development with AI-driven code generation and task automation. You can use Kilo Code as an open source extension in VS Code or JetBrains IDEs.

À TRADUIRE: Organizations adopting AI accelerated coding at scale often want a better way to monitor, manage, and collaborate on their AI-drive practices.

À TRADUIRE: Kilo Code's paid plans, Teams and Enterprise, are the solution for these organizations.

:::note

À TRADUIRE: Purchases of Kilo Code's paid plans are separate from model provider credits.

À TRADUIRE: No credits are included with a Teams or Enterprise plan purchase.

:::

## À TRADUIRE: What You Get from Kilo Teams

- **À TRADUIRE: Zero markup** on AI provider costs - pay exactly what providers charge
- **À TRADUIRE: No rate limiting** or quality degradation during peak usage
- **À TRADUIRE: Centralized billing** - one invoice for your whole team
- **À TRADUIRE: Complete transparency** - see every request, cost, and usage pattern
- **À TRADUIRE: Team management** - roles, permissions, and usage controls

**À TRADUIRE: Cost:** $29 per user per month

## À TRADUIRE: What You Get from Kilo Enterprise

**À TRADUIRE: Everything from Teams** plus...

- **À TRADUIRE: Limit models and/or providers** to control costs and ensure compliance
- **À TRADUIRE: Audit Logs** for enhanced observability
- **À TRADUIRE: SSO, OIDC, & SCIM support**
- **À TRADUIRE: SLA commitments** for support issues
- **À TRADUIRE: Dedicated support channels** for private, direct communication

**À TRADUIRE: Cost:** $299 per user per month
