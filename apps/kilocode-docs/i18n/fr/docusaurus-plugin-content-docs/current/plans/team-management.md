# À TRADUIRE: Managing Your Team

À TRADUIRE: Every person on the team is an _À TRADUIRE: Owner_ or a _À TRADUIRE: Member_.

À TRADUIRE: Owners have full administrative oversight including billing, seat allocation, and model/provider selection.

À TRADUIRE: Only Owners can conduct team management activities.

À TRADUIRE: Members can use the Kilo Code extension and see data on the team's usage in the [À TRADUIRE: usage dashboard](/plans/analytics).

## À TRADUIRE: Adding Team Members

1. **À TRADUIRE: Navigate to Organization Tab** in your profile page and click on the team you want to manage
2. **À TRADUIRE: Click "Invite Member"** button
3. **À TRADUIRE: Enter the team member's email address**
4. **À TRADUIRE: Select initial role** (Member or Owner)
5. À TRADUIRE: Click **À TRADUIRE: Send Invitation**

## À TRADUIRE: Removing Team Members

À TRADUIRE: When team members leave:

1. **À TRADUIRE: Navigate to Organization tab**
2. **À TRADUIRE: Find the departing member**
3. **À TRADUIRE: Click "Remove" button**
4. **À TRADUIRE: Confirm removal**
5. **À TRADUIRE: Seat becomes available** immediately

## À TRADUIRE: Changing Team Member Roles

À TRADUIRE: Promote or demote team members as needed:

1. **À TRADUIRE: Locate team member** in Organization tab
2. **À TRADUIRE: Click role dropdown** next to their name
3. **À TRADUIRE: Select new role** (Member, Owner)
4. **À TRADUIRE: Confirm change**
5. **À TRADUIRE: Member receives email notification**

### À TRADUIRE: Viewing Team Status

À TRADUIRE: The Organization tab shows:

- **À TRADUIRE: Active members** with last activity
- **À TRADUIRE: Pending invitations** awaiting acceptance
- **À TRADUIRE: Role distribution** across the team
